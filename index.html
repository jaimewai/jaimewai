About me

I'm Jaime, a third year dual degree statistics and business student at the University of Toronto. I am fascinated with the intersections between technology and business, and the amazing things that they can create. This lead to my interests in learning more about strategy & analytics, product management, UX/UI design and the finance/technology industry. I hope to use my skills and experiences to create a positive impact on things I work on:)


I enjoy reading, watching sports (NBA, figure skating, gymnastics (including NCAA), and tennis), trying out new restaurants and coffee shops in my city, and travelling when I can. As a student, I love to learn; researching new products in tech and learning different programming languages in my spare time. I love to create conversations and learn about people's stories- Feel free to reach out, I am always open to chat!
